Foreningsportal – Product & Pricing Strategy

1. Product Vision

Build a modern, simple, and scalable platform for associations (“foreninger”) that replaces outdated, complex systems with an intuitive, automation-driven experience.

Positioning:
	•	Simpler than existing systems
	•	More modern UX
	•	Competitive pricing

⸻

2. Core Features (MVP)

2.1 Membership Management
	•	Member database (search, filters, tags)
	•	Self-service profile editing
	•	Online signup and cancellation
	•	GDPR consent handling
	•	Role management (member, admin, coach, board)

2.2 Payments & Billing
	•	Membership subscriptions (monthly/yearly)
	•	One-time payments
	•	Online payments (MobilePay, cards)
	•	Automatic invoicing
	•	Payment reminders

2.3 Activities & Events
	•	Event creation and signup
	•	Recurring activities
	•	Attendance tracking
	•	Waitlists

2.4 Communication
	•	Email notifications
	•	Group-based messaging
	•	Announcements/news

⸻

3. Phase 2 Features (Differentiation)

3.1 Automation Engine
	•	Auto-renew memberships
	•	Trigger-based actions (e.g. payment → confirmation email)
	•	Group assignment rules (age, activity, etc.)

3.2 UX & Onboarding
	•	Mobile-first interface
	•	Easy onboarding flow
	•	Excel import for members

⸻

4. Phase 3 Features (Expansion)

4.1 Booking System
	•	Resource booking (rooms, courts, equipment)
	•	Time slots and availability

4.2 Mobile App
	•	Activity signup
	•	Notifications
	•	Booking access

4.3 Integrations
	•	Accounting systems (e.g. e-conomic)
	•	Access control systems
	•	API for third-party integrations

⸻

5. Advanced / Optional Features
	•	Analytics & reporting (membership trends, attendance)
	•	Sponsor management
	•	Custom forms and workflows
	•	Document storage

⸻

6. Pricing Strategy

6.1 Positioning
	•	Cheaper than premium competitors
	•	Simpler than complex systems
	•	Scalable for both small and mid-sized associations

⸻

6.2 Pricing Tiers

Starter Plan (Free or 29 DKK/month)
	•	Up to 50 members
	•	Basic membership management
	•	Email communication

Purpose: Acquire small clubs and early adoption

⸻

Growth Plan (99–149 DKK/month)
	•	Unlimited members
	•	Payments & billing
	•	Events & activities
	•	Basic automation

Purpose: Core revenue driver

⸻

Pro Plan (299–499 DKK/month)
	•	Full automation engine
	•	Booking system
	•	SMS messaging
	•	API access & integrations

Purpose: Advanced users and scaling organizations

⸻

6.3 Add-ons (High Margin)
	•	SMS credits
	•	Branded mobile app
	•	Accounting integrations
	•	Access control integrations

⸻

7. Go-To-Market Strategy

7.1 Initial Target
	•	Small to medium associations
	•	Sports clubs
	•	Hobby groups

7.2 Key Selling Points
	•	Easy to use
	•	Fast setup
	•	Affordable pricing

⸻

8. Product Strategy Roadmap

Phase 1 (0–3 months)
	•	Membership management
	•	Payments
	•	Events
	•	Email communication

Phase 2 (3–6 months)
	•	Automation engine
	•	Improved UX
	•	Import tools

Phase 3 (6–12 months)
	•	Booking system
	•	Mobile app
	•	Integrations

⸻

9. Key Differentiator

Build a system that feels like a modern SaaS product rather than a traditional administrative tool.

Focus on:
	•	Clean UX
	•	Automation-first workflows
	•	Minimal setup friction

⸻

10. Strategic Direction

Start simple and expand gradually:
	1.	Launch with core features
	2.	Win users with usability
	3.	Expand into advanced features and integrations

Goal: Become the preferred platform for associations by combining simplicity, automation, and affordability.
